Common Queries

How is it different from regular Fat Grafting?

Regular fat grafting adds "volume" to cheeks or chin. Nano Fat is different—it is filtered so finely that it doesn't add bulk; instead, it provides "repair." It’s packed with stem cells that improve skin quality and brighten dark circles.

Is it a permanent solution?

Yes, because it uses your own living cells! Once the fat cells settle, the results are much longer-lasting than temporary chemical fillers. It can keep your under-eye area looking fresh for years.

Is the procedure painful?

We use local anesthesia to numb both the area where we take the fat (usually the belly or thigh) and the face. You will feel very comfortable during the entire process, with only a light pressure sensation.

What is the recovery time?

Since it’s a "mini-procedure," there might be slight swelling or tiny bruises for 3-5 days. Most patients return to their social life within a week with a noticeably fresher and brighter look.
