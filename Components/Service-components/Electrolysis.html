Common Queries

How is it different from Laser?

Laser reduces hair growth and works best on dark hair. Electrolysis actually kills the hair follicle permanently and works on every single hair, regardless of color (even white or blonde).

Does it hurt?

Most patients feel a quick, warm tingle or a slight "pinch" for a second. We use very fine, specialized probes to ensure the process is as comfortable as possible.

How many sessions will I need?

Since hair grows in cycles and we treat each hair one by one, a series of sessions is needed. The exact number depends on the thickness and area of the hair.

Is it safe for the face?

Absolutely. Electrolysis is extremely safe for delicate areas like the chin, upper lips, and eyebrows. There is no long-term damage to the surrounding skin.
