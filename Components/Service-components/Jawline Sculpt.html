Common Queries

How is the jawline sculpted?

We primarily use high-density dermal fillers to add structure and volume to the jaw angle and chin. This creates a sharp edge that separates your face from your neck, giving a more athletic and youthful look.

How long do the results last?

Since the jawline requires thicker, more durable fillers, the results are quite long-lasting—typically between 12 to 18 months depending on your lifestyle and metabolism.

Is there any downtime?

There is almost zero downtime. You might experience very slight swelling or tenderness along the jaw for 2-3 days, but you can resume your daily work immediately after leaving our clinic.

Will it look natural?

Yes! Our experts are trained to follow your natural anatomy. The goal is to make your jawline look "naturally sharp," not artificial or bulky. We tailor the sharpness according to male or female facial standards.
