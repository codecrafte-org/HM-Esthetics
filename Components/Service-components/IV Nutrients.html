Common Queries

Why not just take vitamin pills?

When you swallow a pill, only 20-30% of the nutrients are absorbed by your stomach. With IV Therapy, 100% of the nutrients enter your blood, giving you much faster and more visible results.

How long does one session take?

A typical IV drip session takes about 30 to 45 minutes. You can relax, watch a movie, or use your phone while the nutrients slowly drip into your system.

When will I feel the effects?

Energy levels often improve within a few hours. For skin brightening and detox effects, most patients notice a clearer, fresher look after 3 to 4 sessions.

Is it safe?

Yes, absolutely. All our drips are administered by trained medical professionals using high-quality, sterile ingredients tailored to your body's specific needs.
