Common Queries

Is it painful?

We use local anesthesia to numb the area completely before we start. You might feel a slight pulling or pressure during the procedure, but it is not painful.

How long do the results last?

You will see an instant lift right away. These results typically last between 12 to 18 months as the threads slowly dissolve and leave behind a structure of new collagen.

Is there any downtime?

Most patients return to normal activities within 24-48 hours. You may experience minor swelling or tiny bruises for a few days, but these are easily covered with light care.

Are the threads visible?

Not at all. The threads are placed underneath the skin and are completely invisible. You will only see the beautiful, lifted effect they create.
