Common Queries

Does it hurt?

Before the treatment, we apply a high-quality numbing cream to your face. You might feel a slight vibrating sensation or a "scratchy" feeling in sensitive areas, but it is very tolerable and not painful.

How much downtime is there?

Your skin will look like a mild sunburn for 24 to 48 hours. Most people return to work the very next day. By day 3, the redness usually disappears, revealing a fresh glow.

How many sessions are needed for scars?

For skin brightening, 1-2 sessions are enough. However, for deep acne scars or large pores, we typically recommend a series of 3 to 6 sessions for the best results.

Can I wear makeup after the session?

It’s best to wait at least 24 hours before applying makeup. This gives the tiny micro-channels enough time to close and protects your skin from any irritation or bacteria.
