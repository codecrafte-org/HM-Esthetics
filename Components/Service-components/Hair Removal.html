Common Queries

Is it permanent?

Yes! It significantly reduces hair growth permanently. After a full course, you will only need a very occasional touch-up session maybe once a year.

Does it hurt?

Our Soprano Titanium laser has a cooling tip, making the treatment virtually painless. It feels like a warm massage rather than a snap.

How much does it cost?

Our prices are very affordable, starting from PKR 3,000. For specific areas like the face, it’s around PKR 6,000, and we offer great packages for Full Body too.

How should I prepare?

Please shave the area 24 hours before your session. Avoid waxing or plucking for at least 2 weeks before coming in, as the laser needs the hair root to work.
