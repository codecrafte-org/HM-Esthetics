Common Queries

How long does it take to see results?

Skin whitening is a gradual process. Most people start noticing a "fresher" and brighter look after 3-4 sessions, with significant tone changes appearing after a full course of 8-10 sessions.

Are these drips safe?

Yes, our drips contain antioxidants that are naturally found in the human body. When administered in the correct dosage by our professionals, they are safe and provide a healthy boost to your skin and immune system.

How often should I get a drip?

For the best and fastest results, we usually recommend one drip per week. Once you achieve your desired tone, you can switch to a maintenance drip once a month.

Do the results last forever?

The results are long-lasting, but like any beauty treatment, maintenance is key. Avoiding excessive sun exposure and using a good sunscreen will help you keep your new, brighter tone for a very long time.
