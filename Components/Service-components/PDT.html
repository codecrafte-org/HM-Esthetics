Common Queries

How does Light Therapy work?

The light targets specific cells in your skin. For example, Blue light kills acne bacteria, while Red light helps in healing and reducing inflammation.

Is it painful?

Not at all! It is a very relaxing treatment. You just lie down under the specialized light for 15-20 minutes. There are no needles and no heat.

Is there any downtime?

Zero downtime. Your skin might look slightly pink for an hour, but you can go straight back to work or out with friends immediately.

How many sessions are needed?

For acne, we usually recommend 4 to 6 sessions (one per week) to see a significant and long-lasting clearing of the skin.
