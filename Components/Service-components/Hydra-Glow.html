Common Queries

Is it different from a regular facial?

Yes! Regular facials involve manual scrubbing. Hydra-Glow uses a specialized "vortex" tool that sucks out dirt and pumps in hydration without any irritation or redness.

How long do the results last?

The smooth feel and "glow" are instant and usually stay for 2 to 4 weeks. Regular sessions every month help keep your skin healthy and clear in the long run.

Is there any downtime?

None at all. In fact, your skin will look better the moment you leave the clinic. You can apply makeup or go to an event immediately after the session.

Does it hurt?

Not at all! Many of our clients find it very relaxing. It feels like a cool, wet paintbrush moving slowly over your face.
