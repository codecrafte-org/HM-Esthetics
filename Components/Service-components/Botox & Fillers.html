Common Queries

What is the difference between Botox and Fillers?

Botox "relaxes" the muscles that cause wrinkles (like forehead lines). Fillers "fill in" areas that have lost volume or need shaping (like lips or cheeks). Often, both are used together for a full refresh.

Will I look "fake" or frozen?

Not at HM Aesthetic. We believe in the "Natural Look" policy. We use just the right amount to make you look younger and fresher while keeping your natural facial expressions intact.

How long do the results last?

Botox results typically last 3 to 4 months. Dermal Fillers are longer-lasting, usually visible for 6 to 12 months depending on the area treated.

Is there any downtime?

This is a "lunchtime procedure." It takes about 15-30 minutes. You might have tiny red marks or very slight swelling for a few hours, but you can go back to work immediately.
