Common Queries

What is the difference between Drips and Shots?

Drips are a full bag of fluids that take 40 minutes and provide deep hydration. Shots are concentrated, small-volume injections that take only 2-3 minutes. Shots are excellent as "boosters" or for maintenance between drip sessions.

Are Whitening Shots painful?

Not at all. It is just like a regular vaccination or a small prick. Our trained staff ensures the process is quick, safe, and as comfortable as possible.

How often can I get these shots?

Usually, we recommend one to two shots per week, depending on your skin goal. Our specialists will guide you on the best schedule after looking at your skin type.

Is there any downtime?

Zero downtime. You can walk in, get your shot, and walk out to your meeting or work immediately. There is no recovery time needed.
