Common Queries

Is the procedure painful?

We apply a high-grade topical numbing cream before the treatment, ensuring you feel little to no discomfort during the micro-injections.

How soon can I see results?

Most patients notice a "PRP glow" within the first week, but the real collagen boost peaks at around 3-4 weeks after the session.

Any downtime or side effects?

Since it uses your own blood, there's no risk of allergy. Slight redness or swelling may occur, which typically subsides within 24 hours.

How many sessions are recommended?

For best results, a course of 3 to 4 sessions spaced one month apart is highly recommended by our specialists.
