Common Queries

How is this different from regular bleach?

Regular cream bleach uses chemicals that can itch or burn the skin. Laser Bleaching is chemical-free, much safer, and also helps in cleaning the skin pores while making the hair invisible.

How long does the effect last?

The hair will remain bleached and invisible until it naturally falls out and new hair grows back, which usually takes about 4 to 6 weeks.

Will my hair grow back thicker?

Absolutely not. One of the best things about Laser Bleaching is that it does not stimulate hair growth or make the hair thicker when it returns.

Is it painful?

No, it is a very comfortable procedure. You might feel a tiny tingling sensation as the laser passes over the skin, but it is not painful at all.
