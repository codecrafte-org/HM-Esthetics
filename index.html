Common
Inquiries

How long do the results typically last?

+

Depending on the treatment, results can last from 6 months (for basic hydra-facials) up to 18 months for structural dermal fillers. Dr. Hassan will provide a personalized timeline during your consultation.

Is there any downtime after the procedure?

+

Most of our non-surgical procedures allow you to return to work immediately. Mild swelling or redness may occur but usually subsides within 24 to 48 hours.

Are the treatments painful?

+

We prioritize your comfort. We use high-quality numbing creams and advanced techniques to ensure the experience is as painless and comfortable as possible.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

+

We recommend starting with our "Virtual Skin Analysis" or a 1-on-1 clinical consultation where we assess your skin type and facial anatomy to create a bespoke plan.
